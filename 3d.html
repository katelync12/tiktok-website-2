AP exam overview

The AP exam consists of a portfolio that you create throughout the school year. AP 3-D Art and Design is an introductory college-level three-dimensional design course. Students refine and apply 3-D skills to ideas they develop throughout the course. The course aims to work on three skills:

  1. Inquiry and Investigation

  2. Making Through Practice, Experimentation, and Revision

  3. Communication and Reflection

Collegeboard Past FRQs

Collegeboard has some past portfolios that got different scores. These can help with both inspiration and be used as examples.
